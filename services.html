Our Facility

Intensive Care

At Niramay Hospital we have specialized Intensive Care Units in each department equipped with ultra-sophisticated health care equipment and we have following departments with dedicated intensive care units along with a comprehensive team to care patients in each departments of specialised Intensive Care Units as Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU), Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU) and High-Dependency Unit (HDU) and all the patients under these units are continuously monitored and treated by multiple teams to get fast recovery

Outpatient Services

We house outpatient departments for more than 10 specialties, so that you get the most precise diagnosis and treatment.

Ambulance Service

We provide round-the-clock ambulance services, and our emergency department is fully equipped to handle full-scale trauma and emergency care 24 x 7.

Diagnostics

Niramay Hospital for diagnostics services which offers thousands of cutting-edge laboratory tests, health checks and investigative panels right on campus with most results available on the same day of testing.

Patient Wards

Niramay Hospital has a capacity of more than 50 beds. A patient at our hospital can choose from an array of deluxe, private, semi-private and general ward rooms. All of them offer a comfortable stay, in an environment best suited for holistic healing.

Operation Theaters

We have 2 operating theaters for emergency procedures, labor and delivery and super-specialties such as neurosurgery, pediatric surgery, urology.
