General Medicine

With our expertise and cutting-edge medical technologies we have combined high-quality patient-centered adult therapy, offering not only clinical but also preventive and lifestyle modification counseling. Between 100 and 150 patients are seen every day in the OPD. Effective treatments are provided for infective conditions such as malaria, dengue fever, enteric fever, and gastroenteritis. The OPD also has days to develop a complete healthcare program for Rheumatology, Neurology and Diabetes OPD, and Cardiology OPD. Constant commitment to general medicine. The OPD Center for Victims and Day Care is well-equipped for treating, triaging, and monitoring patients. Life-saving steps are utilized in an emergency, like intubation and defibrillation. Ascitic and pleural membrane tapping Blood gas, sugar, and oxygen analysis analysis There are adequate critical care services with fully equipped ICUs in both the teaching and the super-specialty wings. Intense persons ask for monitored, complete critical care management is provided 24 hours a day, seven days a week. A seamless and consistent connection between pulmonology, radiography, pathology, and microbiology is necessary for accurate, rapid diagnosis and medical care in all other fields.
